MISSION PRARAMBH


On 18 November 2022 the Vikram-S rocket was successfully launched from
Sriharikota by Skyroot Aerospace, turning a page in the history of
Indian space sector, by becoming the first private rocket to be
launched in India and South Asia. While showcasing Skyroot's
exceptional mission agility and technological capabilities to the
world, this incredible achievement put India on the global space
map as a strong global contender in the private space industry.


Read about the remarkable Mission Prarambh that has gone down the
history books, on this page!
